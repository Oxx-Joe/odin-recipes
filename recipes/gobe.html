Gobe



Description

Gari-beans aka borbor(Daavi's yours truly) aka gobe as it is affectionately called, aka yok3-gari is a popular Ghanaian food loved by all due to its affordability, ease of preparation and great taste. It takes about 2 hours to getone with though. It only requires five ingredients. Fun fact: Gobe causes early satiety and prolongs duration of satistafaction hence could help in weight loss

Ingredients

  • Gari
  • Beans
  • Riped plantain
  • Veggies
  • Avocados
  • Red oil

Steps

  1. Fill a pot till it's almost full with water and add your washed beans
  2. Bring it to boil till the beans softens
  3. Blend pepper and onions
  4. Put a silver ware on fire, pour about 100ml of red oil in it and add some sliced onions to it
  5. Empty the pot of beans to the silver ware with the red oil, stir and add salt to taste
  6. Serve and sprinkle 5g of gari on it and garnish it with the avocados
Bon appetite
