Gari Soakings



Description

Gari soakings is a popular Ghanaian food loved especially by students due to its affordability, ease of preparation and great taste. It only requires five ingredients. To all my "lack-toes-in-to-ler-ant" and peanut allergic people, please check out my other recipes.

Ingredients

  • Gari
  • Sugar
  • Whole milk
  • Peanut
  • Water

Steps

  1. Pour about 300ml 0f water preferably chilled water into a cup or bowl
  2. Add sugar to the cup of water to suit your preference
  3. Add about 50ml of whole milk, you can add more to your liking
  4. Throw in 'em peanuts now
  5. Add small quantities of gari say 10g while staring to your desire thickness then enjoy!!!
