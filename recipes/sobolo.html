Sobolo



Description

Sobolo, "aka sodium bolo oxide" just kidding is a drink that will make you fullfilled in life. After taking this drink, you wouldn't want to take any other drink. It is a drink made from dried hibiscus leaves

Ingredients

  • Dried hibiscus leaves
  • water
  • Sugar
  • Flavour

Steps

  1. Bring a liter of water to a boil
  2. Fill a cup with the leaves to about half
  3. Pour the hot water in the cup
  4. Wait about an hour for leaves to allow for maceration
  5. Sieve the mixture
  6. Add sugar and flavouring of your choice, chill the drink and enjoy!!!
